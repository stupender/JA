about

Jon Adams is a songwriter whose songs cover a wide variety of themes in multiple genres.  Stu Pender is a guitarist and producer.  After Jon composes the music and lyrics, Stu produces the songs and they assemble vocalists and other musicians to record demos for publishing or for consideration by artists wishing to perform the songs.   Jon leverages his time as an actor to create songs that are deeply personal and meaningful for a given need or specific vocalist.  Stu shapes ideas in various stages of completion by adding percussion, playing any needed instruments, engineering and otherwise getting the song to completion.
